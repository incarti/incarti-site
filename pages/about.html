About Incarti
Self assembled tech is different to bought tech. No one ever boasts the price of their tree house, or repaired door handle - price and cost dont seem to exist in the realm of the self assembled. Why? We're not too sure. But we like it. Craftsmanship, attitude and tinkering remain in the world of the assembly.

What we do is build parts for you to use - however you want. We dont want to reinvent the wheel - I'm not sure anyone would ever sign themselves up to redesign screws, but what can be improved, documented, and simplied for local production we'll try to look into.
